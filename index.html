About

Calista Ordas is a coterminal student at Stanford University pursuing an M.S. in Earth Systems with a focus on spatial environmental analysis, following her B.S. in Earth Systems and a minor in Data Science. Her work spans environmental research, GIS analysis, and community-focused projects across research, consulting, and NGO settings.

Calista has been recognized with multiple academic awards, including Excellence in Undergraduate Academics and Excellence in Senior Capstone. She currently serves as Head Teaching Assistant for two of the largest courses in the Doerr School of Sustainability and is passionate about applying GIS and spatial analysis to support nonprofit and community-based environmental work.
